Tips for Lost & Found Pets

If Your Pet is Lost:

  • Create a detailed listing with recent photos
  • Include your contact information
  • Mention any distinctive features or behaviors
  • Check local shelters and veterinary clinics
  • Post on social media and community boards

If You Found a Pet:

  • Check for identification tags or microchips
  • Create a found pet listing with photos
  • Contact local shelters and veterinary clinics
  • Post on social media and community boards
  • Keep the pet safe while searching for the owner
